3D Systems: Procedurally Generated Form

industrial design, parametric modelling, 3D printing

In the summer of 2014 I worked at 3D Systems SF. My main project was to develop a series of lighting objects to be printed in a ceramic 3D printer. I developed these designs parametrically through Rhino, Grasshopper, and physical prototyping.

I also spent some time working to address the experience of co-creation applications. This is a mock-up of a UI for co-creation that would allow makers to control the creation of objects through the browser with a cocktail of slider-based input as well as other forms of input, like sound.
