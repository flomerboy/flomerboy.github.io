I began my exploration with play by creating a taxonomy of play. The purpose of creating this taxonomy was to create a personal framework to draw connections within about the nature of play. I started out by referencing this comprehensive list of the types of play.

After watching this TED talk by Stuart Brown, I thought that there must be a simpler way of disecting how play affects the development of the individual. I thought back to the notion that the complete individual is composed of three parts: physical, social, and intellectual.

I took this idea and developed a system to assign colors to playtypes based on the extent to which they exercise the body, ego, and intellect.
